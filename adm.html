The Student Contract

When you accept an offer of a place on a University of Bradford programme (either by accepting us via UCAS as your firm or insurance choice or by confirming directly with the University) you are entering into a contractual relationship with us. It is important that you understand the nature of this contractual relationship and what you are agreeing to by entering into it.

The University has a set of terms and conditions for all students accepting an offer to study on a course here at Bradford. This is called The Student Contract.

This contract, and supporting appendices, set out the terms and conditions which apply when you accept an offer of a place on a programme of study at the University of Bradford.

These terms and conditions represent an agreement between you, our student, and the University.

If you have any questions or concerns about our terms and conditions, you can contact us by email at: admissionsei@bradford.ac.uk before accepting your offer. If you require this information in an alternative format please contact marketing@bradford.ac.uk.

Accepting our offer of a place

When you have decided whether you want to accept or decline this offer, please log on to the Applicant Portal (login details were sent by email when you first applied), view the offer under 'Course Applications' and click the 'Respond to Offer' button to select your response.

If you accept this offer of a place on a Bradford programme a contract will be made between you and the University of Bradford. Once you have accepted the offer you have 14 days in which you can change your mind. If you do change your mind, you will need to contact us in writing by emailing admissionsei@bradford.ac.uk. After the 14 day period, it may be possible to change your response to this offer, but you will need to contact us to arrange this.

Visa information

Please note that this is your academic offer only. In order for you to enrol on your programme at the University of Bradford you will need to provide us with evidence that you have a valid visa to study in the UK. If you need to apply for a student visa you will need a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS).

The Visa Support Team at the University of Bradford will contact you about your CAS application after you have made your advanced payment. It is your responsibility to check what the pre-CAS requirements are on our website and meet the criteria within the specified deadlines. Failure to do so could lead to you not getting a CAS.

Please see the UK Visas & Immigration website for further advice about applying for a student visa: www.gov.uk/student-visa.

 Accommodation and Living Expenses

If you would like to apply for a place in University-approved accommodation (single study bedroom suitable for unaccompanied students), please see our website for further details: www.bradford.ac.uk/accommodation.

The University of Bradford provides an estimate of minimum recommended living allowances for a single student in the Postgraduate Applicant Information document. However, the Home Office will REQUIRE you to provide proof of a MINIMUM of 9207 GBP for living expenses (for self-funding students) plus the tuition fees in order for you to be eligible for a student visa. Our International Student Support & UKVI Compliance Team will be able to advise you about your specific financial requirement to apply for the student visa.
