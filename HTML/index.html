Why Kreative Street?

We are more than a training platform. We are your career partner. Your success is our business.

We are that career buddy you need to excel in your chosen tech career. Through our trainings, mentorships, internships etc, we will be with you every step of the way

Internship Asistance

We know how discouraging the job market can be especially for entry level roles. Hence, we are committed to working with you to land your first tech role. Although, we are not guranteeing you an internship but we won't stop until you get one.

1:1 Mentorship

To help you excel at your first job, you will be matched to a mentor relevant to your industry. You will have the opportunity to get all the help, answers and resources that will help you meet the demands of your position. You definitely will not be walking alone.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

As product roles demand accountability and teamwork, we'll immerse you in the collaborative experience by pairing you with fellow trainees throughout the three-month program. You'll have the chance to closely collaborate with three different teammates, honing your skills in real-world scenarios.

Thriving Community

You have a lifetime access to our community which means a lifetime learnings. As an alumni in our community, you get unlimited and firsthand access to templates, tools, events and trendy industry news.
